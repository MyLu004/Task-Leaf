Pomodoro Cycle:


  • Work Interval: Typically 25 minutes, focused work on a specific task.
    • - Short Break: A 5-minute break to relax and recharge.
    • - Pomodoro Completion: After completing four work intervals, take a longer break of around 15-30 minutes.

    More info about pomodoro technique

  • Icon function: Each icon will toggle active eveytime you finish the focus [work] cycle, as long you have 4 icons, the next auto cycle break will be long break.

  • Sound effect: Checkbox to turn the functions off. The sound effect is play sound when button click or when complete tasks,break end. If the sound is distracted, feel free to turn it off :3

  • Time setting: Default is 25,05,15 for focus, short break, long break to follow the podomoro method. But feel free to adjust the time as you prefer
